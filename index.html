Welcome! I'm currently an undergraduate student in School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China. Starting in September 2023, I will be a PhD student at the Institute of Automation , Chinese Academy of Sciences(CAS), advised by Professor Chengqing Zong.

My research interests broadly lie in computational linguistics and natural language processing.

I have passions for a wide variety of fields, and I am constantly exploring new areas and possibilities. Some of my major interests include classical music (especially thanks to Ludwig van Beethoven who provides me with immense power when I'm feeling down), swimming and philosophy of language .

Feel free to ping me if you are interested as well!

Bio

I primarily grew up in Lu'an, China. From 2017-2018, I studied Chinese Physics Olympiad. From 2019-2020 I studied Geophysics at USTC, until I became interested in Computer Science and changed my major to it in July 2020.

During my undergrad period, I had been interning in the BDAA lab, supervised by Prof. Qi Liu and Prof. Enhong Chen. I was deeply involved in developing an advanced NLP library towards multi-modal educational items. We (several of the lab members) won a Silver Medal (top 100/2000) in a Kaggle competition of evaluating student writing .

I was a member of USTC Omni Media Center, executing official social media.
